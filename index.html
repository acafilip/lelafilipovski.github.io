Clay knows the mystery of time...

There are millions of years encapsulated in every particle of clay. When working with it, I feel that the answer regarding time presents itself to me; it is embedded within my work, inspired by the texture and natural color of clay. Making a statement in words is superfluous to me – I let the clay make my ultimate statement, most true to what I feel as an artist.
